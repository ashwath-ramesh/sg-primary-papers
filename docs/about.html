About

A clean index of Primary 1, Primary 2, Primary 3 papers, plus Primary 4 Maths and Primary 5 Maths, with filters and source links.

We do not host or mirror PDFs. Each item points to its source.
Note: in the Singapore syllabus, Science typically starts from Primary 3, so P1/P2 Science papers are uncommon.

No ads. No popups. For corrections or takedown requests, open a GitHub issue.
