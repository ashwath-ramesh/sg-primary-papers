Where to get past year exam papers in Singapore (Primary)

Short answer: there’s no single “official mega-folder”. The best approach is a curated index + reliable source links + realistic expectations.

Good sources (practical)

  • Curated paper indexes (fastest): use an index like this site to filter by level/subject/year.
  • Parent communities (hit-or-miss): useful for leads, but links expire and quality varies.
  • Bookstores / printed bundles: convenient if your child prefers paper, but costs add up.

What to check before you trust a paper

  • Is the school + year clearly stated?
  • Is it a test/quiz/SA (different difficulty expectations)?
  • Does it include answers (and are they correct)?
