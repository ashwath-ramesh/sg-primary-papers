Free vs paid exam papers — what’s worth it?

Free papers are often enough for coverage. Paid bundles mainly sell convenience: curation, completeness, and time saved.

Free is enough if…

  • You can consistently find papers for your level/subject/year.
  • You’re willing to spend time filtering, downloading, and organising.

Paid can be worth it if…

  • You want a complete set (by year/term) with minimal searching.
  • You need answer keys and clear labeling (saves parent time).
  • Your child prefers printed packs.

What to avoid

  • Unclear provenance (no school/year/assessment details).
  • Mislabelled difficulty (demoralising sets sold as “must do”).
